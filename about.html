About me

I am an Australian medical doctor, working as a basic physician trainee, with aspirations of participating in the Geriatric and Longevity medicine space.

I am passionate about a holistic approach to health and wellness, and think that everyone should develop their own personal framework to take on life with.

About the information on this website

This website has some general information for education purposes only, specifically on the topic of wellbeing.

It's a brief introduction to my opinion around some of the internal architecture of a good life.

The way you think, eat, move, recover, relate to others, manage stress, and structure your habits determines far more than most people realise.

This website's ethos is not about perfection. It's about living a noble life in the sense of Ernest Hemingway's profound quote:

"There is nothing noble in being superior to your fellow man; true nobility is being superior to your former self."
— Ernest Hemingway

It's about building a stronger baseline and the journey of self-improvement — one decision, one habit, one iteration at a time.

Gathering building blocks in mental, emotional, social and physical domains can strengthen your constitution.

There is an inexplicable duality between them all that steady's the sail, guides the hand at the helm, and lights the horizon on the ship we each build to circumnavigate life.

How does your boat look?

Perhaps this website has something useful for its frame (pun intended!).
